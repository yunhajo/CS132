Rules

Welcome to Match the Cards! Your goal is to match all of the cards in the given time. You can choose between 30 seconds, 60 seconds, and 90 seconds.

Choose a time, then click on start button to start the game. You will be shown a 4x4 deck of cards, randomly shuffled.

Once you click on a card, it will flip backwards to show you the front of the card. Click on two of the cards to see if they match. If they match, then they will disappear from the board. If they don't then they will flip back after a second.

Your job is to match all 8 sets of cards in the given time.

If you want to restart you game, click on "Back to Home" button at the bottom of the screen then start again!
